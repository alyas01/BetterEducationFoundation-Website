Letter from Our Team

Our goals for The Better Education Foundation are: to provide support, strengthen and expose both women and children, here in America and around the world to educational opportunity. Having backgrounds as both first generation Americans and African immigrants, our experiences have propelled us to begin stressing the importance of access to education for all. As college students, we are determined to provide selfless service to improve the quality of education. The truth is, being able to change the life trajectory of a child is much more tangible when you make it personal. The perspective of why we are all dedicated to this mission is not to better ourselves, but to better the world around us.
